Welcome to your Power Monitoring Dashboard! This system tracks your power consumption in real-time and provides comprehensive analytics to help you understand your energy usage patterns.

üìä Understanding the Metrics

  • Live Power: Real-time power consumption in Watts (W).
  • Average Power: Mean power consumption over the selected time period.
  • Total Energy: Cumulative energy consumption in kilowatt-hours (kWh).
  • Power Cuts: Detected outages when consumption drops to near-zero for a set duration.
  • Est. Cost: An estimation of electricity cost based on total energy and a configurable rate.
